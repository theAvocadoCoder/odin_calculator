Simple Calculator

Tips for Use

  • You can use the numbers and operators on your physical keyboard
  • Pressing the 'Spacebar' key will erase your last input
  • Holding the 'Shift' key while pressing the 'Spacebar' key will clear the display
  • Pressing the '=' key will show the result of your calculation
  • Pressing the 'Enter' key will also show the result of your calculation
